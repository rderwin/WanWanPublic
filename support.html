What DrawJapanese Does

DrawJapanese is a kana handwriting practice app for learning and reviewing Japanese characters. The app includes kana study, drawing practice, quizzes, and progress tracking.

Common Help Topics

  • Handwriting recognition feedback during kana practice
  • Saved progress, quiz history, and drawing history
  • Sound effects and Japanese pronunciation playback
  • General app issues, bugs, or feature requests

When You Email

Please include your device model, iOS version, and a short description of the problem so support can respond more effectively.
