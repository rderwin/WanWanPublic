What WanWan! Does

WanWan! is a voice-first Japanese learning app with 37 structured lessons. The app teaches through listening, speaking, and hands-free quizzes — covering greetings, daily life, food, travel, and more. It also includes handwriting recognition for kana practice, a reference library, verb conjugations, and collectible rewards.

Common Help Topics

  • Microphone not working — Make sure microphone and speech recognition permissions are enabled in Settings → WanWan!
  • Speech not recognized correctly — Try speaking clearly and at a normal pace. The app listens for 1-2 seconds of silence before evaluating.
  • Handwriting not recognized — Draw each stroke in the correct order, following the ghost guide. Clear and try again if needed.
  • Progress not saving — Progress is saved automatically. Make sure you complete each module to save your progress.
  • No sound — Check that your device is not on silent mode and the volume is up.
  • App crashes or freezes — Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, please email us with your device model and iOS version.

When You Email

To help us resolve your issue quickly, please include:

  • Your device model (e.g. iPhone 15 Pro)
  • Your iOS version (e.g. iOS 18.3)
  • A short description of the problem
  • Steps to reproduce the issue, if possible
