Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to DrawJapanese.

Information DrawJapanese Uses

DrawJapanese is designed to work primarily on your device. Based on the current app implementation, the app stores practice-related information locally on the device, including:

Progress records for kana you have practiced, quiz session history, saved drawing attempts, recognition confidence values, timestamps, and your sound preference setting.

Handwriting Recognition

When you draw kana in the app, the drawing is processed on-device using Apple frameworks and bundled machine learning models to evaluate practice attempts. Your handwriting data is not sent to a remote server by the app’s current implementation.

Personal Information

DrawJapanese does not require account creation and does not ask you to provide your name, phone number, mailing address, or other direct contact details in order to use the app.

Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

Based on the current app implementation, DrawJapanese does not include third-party advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or app-based tracking for advertising purposes.

Data Sharing

DrawJapanese does not share your locally stored practice data with third parties through the app’s current implementation.

Data Storage

Practice history and related records are stored locally on your device. If you remove the app, locally stored app data may also be removed by iOS.

Children’s Privacy

DrawJapanese does not knowingly collect personal information from children through the app’s current implementation.

Your Choices

You can stop using the app at any time and may remove the app from your device if you no longer want locally stored app data kept on that device.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated if the app’s features or data practices change. Any updated version should be posted with a revised effective date.
